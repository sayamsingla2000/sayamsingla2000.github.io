I am a AI4Code Research Fellow at Microsoft Research, where I work with Dr. Aditya Kanade, Dr. Navin Goyal, Dr. Shuvendu Lahiri, and Dr. Sriram Rajamani.

Currently, my research focuses on improving the quality of code generated by LLMs aiming to improve their reliability for software engineering tasks. Most recently, I have been focussing on repository-level reasoning for code generation with LLMs.

Keywords: Large Language Models, AI4Code, Code Generation, Static Analysis, Software Engineering

I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics from IIIT-Delhi, India in 2022. For more details about my background, refer to my CV.
