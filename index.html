I am a VeLLM Research Intern at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Dr. Sunayana Sitaram on evaluating multilingual capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs). My research centers around assessing and improving performance of LLMs for low-resource languages to promote fairness and accesibility of AI. I have worked on projects focusing on extensively benchmarking these capabilities and studying efficient fine-tuning techniques to enhance them. Prior to this, I have worked with Dr. Akshay Nambi and Dr. Tanuja Ganu on Shiksha Copilot, an educational AI assistant released by Microsoft.

Currently, my research focuses on developing an ecosystem to fairly evaluate the rising number of Indic LLMs. This platform can serve as a useful reference point for individuals/organisations when building their own LLMs. Additionally, I am collaborating with Dr. Adrian de Wynter from Microsoft Research Redmond on a Responsible AI study.

Keywords: Large Language Models, Multilinugality, AI for Social Good, Responsible AI

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics with a minor in Computer Science from IIT Delhi, India in 2023. In the past, I have interned under Dr. Saiful Islam at Griffith University , where I focused on malware detection using ML. This project involved creating a dataset from binaries, analyzing program structures, and successfully implementing a Graph Convolutional Network. As a Data Scientist at Udaan, I developed a user embedding generation framework, enhancing their fraud detection by 2.45%. I did this by creating a multi-relational and multi-entity interaction graph and applying HeteroGNNs. At Torch Investment Management, I honed my ML engineering skills by working on stock price prediction models and integrating NLP for analyzing market sentiment. For my bachelor's thesis at IIT Delhi, I worked with Prof. Abhishek Iyer on particle physics projects, utilizing ML for anomaly detection and particle identification, exemplifying my ability to apply ML in various fields.

For more details about my background, refer to my CV. If you'd like to chat with me about my work or research in general, feel free to reach out via email!
